# User Testing Summary

Two participants tested the prototype for a few minutes and were asked to perform simple tasks such as searching for recipes and applying dietary filters.

## Participant 1

The participant found the interface clean and visually easy to understand.

They particularly liked the recipe cards and the quick modal view for recipe details.

However, they suggested adding stronger visual feedback when search results update.

## Participant 2

The participant liked the filtering system and felt the categories were intuitive.

They mentioned that the interaction felt smooth but initially did not realize the recipe cards were clickable.

## Key Findings

- Users liked the clean and simple layout
- Recipe cards were easy to scan
- Filtering was intuitive
- Interaction feedback could be improved
